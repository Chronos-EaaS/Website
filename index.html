The Swiss Army Knife for Systems Evaluations

Monitor the status of your evaluations

With Chronos you can monitor the progress of your evaluation from everywhere and at any time. You can easily restarted failed jobs or review intermediate results.

Instant analysis of results within Chronos

The integrated analysis features allow to instantly visualize the result of your evaluation. With various predefined chart types, Chronos is also prepared to visualize the result of sophisticated evaluation scenarios.

Supporting multiple systems at the same time

One instance of Chronos supports you with all your projects at the same time. Thanks to multi-user support, you can even share it with you colleagues or a whole lab.
